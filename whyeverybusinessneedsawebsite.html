Why Every Business Needs a Website

By Sharon Mutanu • May 2025

Let’s be real—if your business isn’t online in 2025, you’re basically invisible. I said what I said.

1. It’s Your 24/7 Storefront

Your website works while you sleep. It welcomes customers, shows them what you offer, answers questions, and says, “Hey, we’re legit.” All day. All night.

2. Builds Trust & Credibility

People Google *everything* before they buy. No website? That’s a red flag. A sleek, simple site tells people, “We know our stuff. You’re in good hands.”

3. You Control the Narrative

Social media is nice, but your website? That’s your kingdom. You decide what people see, read, feel. You tell your story, your way.

4. Boosts Visibility

With SEO (search engine optimization), your site can pop up when someone searches for “thrift stores in Nairobi” or “affordable web design.” Free marketing, baby.

5. Converts Curious Clicks into Customers

Want bookings? Orders? DMs that actually lead to sales? A well-designed website guides visitors like, “Hey, click here to buy.” Simple. Smart. Smooth.

Bottom line: If you’ve got a business, you need a website. Not next year. Not “when things pick up.” Now.
